FAQ

Who is Aman Mundra?

Aman Mundra is the Founder & CEO of Welzin and Co-founder & CEO of CogNerd, an ex-PayPal machine learning engineer with 10+ years in ML, based in Chandigarh, India. He is legally Amandeep Singh; goes by Aman; publishes as Aman Mundra.

What is Welzin?

Welzin is a full-stack AI and data firm founded by Aman Mundra — data engineering, machine learning, and GenAI systems delivered end to end. Its products include Startin (a founder roadmap for Indian startups) and an AI/data bootcamp.

What is CogNerd?

CogNerd is an AEO/GEO brand-intelligence platform co-founded by Aman Mundra and Sakshi Dhawan. It tracks how AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity and Gemini cite and describe brands, and scores that visibility as a GEO Score.
